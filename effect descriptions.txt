Give HP
10  Heals for between 10% and 100% HP in increments of 10%. It can't go above Brian's maximum health.
Take HP
10  Hurts for between 10% and 100% HP in increments of 10%. If his HP falls to 0, he won't die until he takes another hit.
Give MP
10  Recovers between 10% and 100% MP in increments of 10%. It can't go above Brian's maximum MP.
Take MP
10  Drains between 10% and 100% MP in increments of 10%.
Increase Agility
10  Increases Brian's agility by up to 5. It can't go above 512. Agility decreases the chance enemies will hit, increases the chance Brian will hit, determines turn order and determines how far Brian can move in battle.
Decrease Agility
20  Decreases Brian's agility by up to 5. It can't go below 1. Agility decreases the chance enemies will hit, increases the chance Brian will hit, determines turn order and determines how far Brian can move in battle.
Increase Defense
10  Increases Brian's defense by up to 5. It can't go above 512. Defense reduces the damage Brian takes.
Decrease Defense
20  Decreases Brian's defense by up to 5. It can't go below 1. Defense reduces the damage Brian takes.
